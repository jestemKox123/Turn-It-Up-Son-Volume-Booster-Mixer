What it does

More than a volume booster

A per-tab booster and a full audio mixer in one popup. Boost one tab while the rest stay normal.

🔊

Up to 700%, per tab

Push one tab way past the system max while every other tab plays at its normal level.

🥁

Bass boost

Three flavors (Classic, Sub, Punch) with harmonic saturation, so the low end is felt even on small speakers.

🎧

8D audio

The sound orbits around your head. Put headphones on and feel it move.

🎚️

Full mixer

Sliders for speed, reverb, brightness, muffle, bass, vocal, power and spin. Flip one switch to reveal them all.

🎯

Fix one-ear audio

Mixes a lopsided stereo track back to the center so both ears get the sound.

🛡️

Clean limiter

On heavy boost the output is smoothly pushed down instead of crackling and clipping.

Remix any song, live
