Everything in one popup

Small window.
Serious sound engine.

Per-tab volume, up to 700%

Boost one tab while the others play at normal level. Green ON badge shows where it is working. Settings survive a page refresh.

Full mixer

Speed, reverb, brightness, muffle, bass, vocal, power and 8D rotation. Dial in your sound and save it as your own named mix.

Six bass flavors

Classic, Sub, Punch, Rumble, 808 and Warm. From a cinematic roar you feel in your chest to a drill boom that cuts through laptop speakers.

8D audio

The sound smoothly orbits your head. Best with headphones, with a slider for how fast it spins.

Fix one-ear sound

Some uploads play only in the left or right channel. One switch mixes both channels to the center so you hear everything in both ears.

Every playing tab, listed

The popup shows every tab with audio and each one toggles on or off with a single click. Turning it back on restores your last settings.

Auto-skip songs you hate

Blacklist artists, channels or single tracks. When one starts on YouTube, YouTube Music, Spotify or SoundCloud, the next song plays by itself.

Make it yours

Color themes, an interface in English or Polish, your own saved mixes and a max volume cap if 700% is more than you need.

Privacy

Nothing leaves
your device. Ever.

Loud, not nosy

Everything happens locally in your browser.

  • Zero access to websites by default
    It only asks when you turn on a feature that needs it, and gives the access back after.
  • No servers, no analytics, no accounts
    Your audio never leaves the browser. Nothing is collected, nothing is sent.
  • One button wipes all extension data
    Settings, lists and mixes live only on your computer, and you can clear them anytime.
